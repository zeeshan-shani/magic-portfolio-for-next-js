## My Frontend Philosophy

Every pixel matters. Whether it's a large SaaS dashboard, a real-time chat app, or an e-commerce platform, I focus on delivering frontend experiences that are not just functional but *delightful* to users.

I build interfaces that are lightning-fast, scalable, responsive, and visually stunning — using a solid foundation of **Next.js**, **React.js**, **Remix**, **Tailwind CSS**, **Material UI**, **Shadcn**, and more.

## Styling and Theming at Scale

Working with tools like **TailwindCSS**, **Shadcn UI**, and **custom design systems**, I create flexible UI foundations that easily adapt to brand needs — from typography, to color systems, to micro-animations.

theme:      'light',          // Seamless dark/light theme switching
